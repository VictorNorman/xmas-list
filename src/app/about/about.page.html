Welcome to the Gift Lists App!

Author: Victor Norman
Associate Professor of Computer Science
at Calvin University

Instructions: The idea with this app is that each person in your family can share your gift lists with each other in one place. You each put your own gift list into the app, and others can see what you've put it, and you can see others' gift lists. Others can comment on your list, claim items from the list, etc., but you cannot see any of this. You can even put items on others' lists -- but they cannot see those additions.

You can include with each gift a description, cost, picture, and a URL.

Version: 0.1.6
