Welcome to the Gift Lists App!

Author: Victor Norman
Associate Professor of Computer Science
at Calvin University

Instructions: The idea with this app is that each person in your family can share their gift lists with each other in one place. You each put your own gift list into the app, and others can see what you've put in, and you can see others' gift lists. Others can comment on your list, claim items from the list, add items to your list, etc., but you cannot see any of this.

You can include with each gift a description, cost, picture, and a URL.

Login and privacy: Your login information (username, email, and password) is used only to uniquely identify you in the system. The author and administrator of this app can see your login and username information, but cannot read your password -- it is stored in an encrypted format. Nevertheless, it is best to use a unique password when creating your account.
Your personal information will never be given away or sold. Who's got time for those kinds of shinanigans?
